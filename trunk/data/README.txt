The present dataset cover the top 500 longest Articles for Deletion (AfD)
discussions. These data have been used to generate the visualization you can see
at www.notabilia.net. 

Each discussion is characterized by 4 fields:
* name, i.e. the title of the article
* decision, which is either K (page was either kept, merged or redirected) or D (page deleted)
* sequence, the actual sequence of votes, following the above binary (K/D) coding. 
* numKeep, numDelete
